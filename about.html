Benson is a dynamic and impassioned individual, deeply committed to the betterment of his community, particularly the residents of Mumbuni North. His dedication to education, healthcare, land ownership, infrastructure, agriculture, youth empowerment, gender equality, disability inclusion, and sports development reflects a comprehensive understanding of the multifaceted needs of his constituents. As an advocate for education, Benson recognizes that investing in early childhood development centers and ensuring they are staffed with trained personnel is crucial for laying the foundation for future generations. He also acknowledges the critical importance of accessible healthcare, particularly for mothers and children, and vows to lobby for well-equipped dispensaries in every village within the ward. Benson's proactive approach extends to addressing issues surrounding land ownership and accessibility to public roads, recognizing their significance in fostering economic opportunities and community safety. Moreover, his initiative to engage the agricultural department to support residents in commercial farming endeavors demonstrates a forward-thinking approach to economic empowerment. His dedication to youth empowerment is evident through his commitment to securing job opportunities and access to credit facilities for young people. Similarly, his support for women's groups and persons living with disabilities underscores his commitment to inclusivity and equity.

Benson's vision for community development extends to sports, recognizing its role in promoting physical and mental well-being. By advocating for improved sports facilities and resources, he aims to nurture local talent and strengthen community bonds. Above all, Benson emphasizes integrity and accountability in leadership, pledging transparency and responsiveness to the needs of his constituents. His unwavering commitment to serving as a voice for the people of Mumbuni North is characterized by his pledge to operate an open office and uphold the principles outlined in Chapter Six of the Kenyan Constitution. In summary, Benson emerges as a dedicated and visionary leader, driven by a genuine desire to uplift his community and create a brighter future for all its residents.
